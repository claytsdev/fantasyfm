Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

What we collect

FantasyFM collects your Twitch or YouTube username and user ID when you authenticate. This is used solely to identify you within game sessions.

How we use it

Your data is used to display your picks and scores on the leaderboard during live sessions. We do not sell your data to third parties.

Data retention

Session data is retained in our database. You may request deletion by contacting us.
