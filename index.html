FantasyFM brings Fantasy Football to Football Manager live streams. Streamers import their squad before kick-off, viewers pick one defender, one midfielder, one attacker and a captain — then earn points based on how those players perform in the match.

For streamers
Go to Setup and upload a screenshot of your FM squad. Confirm positions, go live, and share your session code on stream.
For viewers
Go to Viewer, enter the session code from the stream, pick one defender, one midfielder, one attacker and a captain — then track your score live.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

FantasyFM ("we", "us") is operated by Clayts. This policy explains how we collect and use your data when you use fantasyfm.io.

Data we collect

When you sign in with Twitch or YouTube, we receive your public username and profile picture. We store this to identify you in game sessions. We do not store your password. When streamers purchase access, Stripe processes payment details. We store game session data including player picks, scores and session codes in Supabase (EU-hosted).

How we use your data

Your data is used solely to operate FantasyFM. We do not sell your data or use it for advertising.

Your rights (GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct or delete your personal data. Contact us via Discord. We will respond within 30 days.
